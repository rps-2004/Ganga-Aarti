About Ganga Aarti Ceremonies

Ganga Aarti is one of the most significant rituals performed daily on the banks of the sacred Ganga River in Varanasi. The Ganga Aarti ceremony is a mesmerizing spectacle of devotion where priests perform ritualistic worship with lamps, incense, and sacred chants. This ancient ritual has been performed for centuries at Dashashwamedh Ghat and represents the spiritual essence of Hindu traditions.
